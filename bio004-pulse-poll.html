Mid-Term Pulse Check

Your honest read on the schedule, your tools, and how it is going

Takes about three minutes. There are no wrong answers. The more honest you are, the more useful this is to me.

This is anonymous. I do not collect your name, email, or any way to identify you. I only see the answers, never who wrote them. Say what you actually think.
1. The weekly rhythm

Watch videos online before class, come in for active learning (cases, drawing, application), TBL on Thursdays, lab exam on Mondays. How do you feel about that structure?

2. Pace and exams, the real tradeoff

If you had to pick one for a course like this, which do you prefer? I cannot promise to change the schedule once the term has started, but I want to know where you stand.

3. Which study tools have you actually used?

Check all that apply. Be honest, even if the answer is "barely any yet."

4. Which have been the most helpful?

Check all that apply. This is the one I care about most, so pick what genuinely moved the needle for you.

5. How are you feeling about the course right now?

Pick the one closest to your honest gut read this week.

6. Hours per week studying outside class

Roughly, on average. No judgment, just a real number.

7. How do you keep your course materials organized?

Check all that apply. The examples are just to make it quick to answer.

8. What could you have done better so far?

Just for your own reflection. Optional, but worth a sentence if you can.

9. Anything else you want me to know?

Optional. The floor is yours, this is the place to be candid.
